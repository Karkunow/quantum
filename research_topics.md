## Quantum Entanglement Deep Dives

### 1. CHSH Inequality 🔍
- **Priority**: High
- **What to explore**:
  - Mathematical derivation of the CHSH inequality (classical bound ≤ 2)
  - How to construct the optimal measurement angles (0°, 45°, 22.5°, 67.5°)
  - Why quantum mechanics violates it (reaching 2√2 ≈ 2.828)
  - Historical experiments: Aspect (1982), Weihs (1998), Hensen (2015)
  - Connection to Bell's original inequality
  - Loopholes: detection, locality, freedom-of-choice
- **Related reading**: 
  - Clauser, Horne, Shimony, and Holt (1969) original paper
  - Aspect's experiments closing locality loophole
  - 2022 Nobel Prize in Physics (Aspect, Clauser, Zeilinger)
